About Us

Alliance Protective Group originated based on the idea that would collectively bring a group of those who specialize in all areas of protective services together, using those resources to provide every client who we enter in to an alliance with the highest quality of personalized service available in the industry. We provide a no-commitment security evaluation free of charge, discussing with you our findings and recommendations. Not all security situations require security officers.
